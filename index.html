How it works

From raw files to
clean video in 5 steps

1

Drop your files

Drag-and-drop your camera video and external mic audio onto the app. Waveforms appear instantly.

2

Auto Sync

Click "Auto Sync" — the app finds the timing offset between the two tracks. Fine-tune with the slider if needed.

3

Detect Silence

Click "Detect Silence" to mark all pauses on the timeline. Adjust the threshold to your preference.

4

Preview

Watch the result live with silences skipped. Toggle individual segments if anything looks off.

5

Export

Hit export. Get a clean MP4 with synced external audio, all silences removed, normalized to YouTube spec.

For anyone who records camera video and external microphone audio separately and wants to save time before editing or publishing — YouTubers, coaches, teachers, podcasters, and content creators.

Currently macOS only (macOS 12 Monterey or later). Windows support is not available at this time.

It works with any external microphone audio file (.WAV or similar). It was optimized for Shure mics, but any external mic audio works.

Never. SyncCut Studio runs 100% locally. No uploads, no accounts, no servers.
